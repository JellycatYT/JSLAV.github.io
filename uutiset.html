    What is JSLAV.GAMES.FI?

    Welcome to JSLAV.GAMES.FI!
    You might be wondering, what is JSLAV.GAMES.FI? Well, let me answer that for you!

    JSLAV was created by five friends who started this journey by selling pencils and erasers at school. Yes, it's crazy, but that’s how it all began!

    We named it JSLAV after the first letter of each of our names. Then one day, I thought, what if we create games? It would be so cool if we could become popular, make awesome games, and maybe even get rich from it! And that's how we ended up here.
